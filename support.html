FAQs

How do I book a tour?

You can book a tour directly on our website by selecting your preferred destination and following the booking instructions.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept M-PESA, Airtel Money and bank transfers. See below for more details.

Can I cancel or modify my booking?

Yes, you can cancel or modify your booking according to our cancellation policy.

How do I contact customer support?

You can reach us via email at support@ancenttours.com or call us at +254701432460.

Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. We collect only necessary data to process your bookings and improve our services. Your information is never shared with third parties without your consent. For more details, please read our full privacy policy on our website.

  • Bank Transfer

Cancellation Policy

  • Cancellations made 7 days before the tour date are eligible for a full refund.

  • No-shows are non-refundable.
